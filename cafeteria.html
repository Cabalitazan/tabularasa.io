Tabula Rasa University

*Cafeteria*

     Students can eat subsidised student meals at the student cafeterias around the campus area. The state subsidises a part of the cost of each student lunch. The student lunch includes: portion of food, glass of drink, salad and bread. Students can eat subsidised student meals 2,60 euros at the student cafeterias around the campus area by showing valid student card. The state subsidises a part of the cost of each student lunch.
