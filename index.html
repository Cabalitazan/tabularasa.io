About

I was born in Agoo, Tubao La Union. I studied at the Tubao Central School for my primary education until 6th grade. I made a lot of friends during my elementary days and until now, I'm still in contact in some of them.

I attended San Alberto Magno Academy which is a Catholic school in my high school. I gained a lot of experiences and knowledge during my time in there. I get to be with some of my friends back at elementary classmates during high school which is pretty awesome.

Right now, I'm already in college. I'm taking Bachelors of Science in Information Technology at the University of the Cordilleras. My journey is still on the go but I can tell right now that it's pretty awesome to be here!
